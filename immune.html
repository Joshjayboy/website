Functional Medicine Testing for Immune System Status

We use the science of integrative and functional medicine to help you determine what your current health status is and what to do now to keep your immune system healthy.

In order to properly assess where you are now we start with a comprehensive history. If needed, we can do specialty lab testing to more accurately interpret your current immune status as well as your general health. (Some lab testing can be performed from home without the need for a blood draw, and we also have mobile lab services available.)

Once we get an accurate picture of your immune health we can customize a treatment plan that is right for you to boost immune health and may include:

  • Foods and dietary protocols to boost immune health: lemons, ginger, bone broth, garlic, and more

  • Vitamins using professional grade nutraceuticals such as Vitamin C, Vitamin A, D3, Zinc

  • Lifestyle practices for stress management, hydration, sleep, laughter and more

  • Supplements such as throat spray, probiotics and many more targeted to you
