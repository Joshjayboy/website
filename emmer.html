Emergency Dental Care from Holistic & Biological Dentists

What does a biological dentist have to offer the emergency dental patient? Biologic dentistry applies two standards of care: the standards of traditional dentistry and the biological dental standard of care, where the mouth is connected to the function of the body as a whole. This means that in every dental procedure and diagnosis both standards apply.

Diagnosis- As an example, in diagnosis of a tooth emergency, the biological, or holistic emergency dentist will try to prevent a root canal unless the tooth is truly irreversibly abscessed. If that is the case, the patient is fully informed of the possible consequences of a root canal.

Treatment- The treatment approach of the biological dentist may differ in that he or she will use drugs when necessary but has more options in the tool box to eliminate the pain, swelling and anxiety that are common with a dental emergency.
